Why do we need to talk about sex?

While bringing up the word "sex" in casual conversation can be considered inappropriate,
there's no reason that learning more about it should be taboo.
It's important that we destygmatize the idea of sex as merely a dirty topic of conversation.
We must be willing to step outside our comfort zones and talk about the
positive and negative realities of a perfectly normal occurence.

Safe sex is smart sex.
